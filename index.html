This Is What It Looks Like In Practice

A founder was looking for a way to invite former clients back to her club. She’d planned a reunion webinar and hadn’t figured out how to optimize her pitch. In 90 minutes together, plus a follow-on recommendation, the strategy I recommended got the desired result:

Do individual outreach to understand the real reason (it’s not always money) they left and/or might not return. And let them experience the community again for one month at no charge as an act of radical generosity. It makes the decision easier, gives her a month to remind them how valuable the club is, and allows them to fall in love with her all over again.

That process became an easier path to renewal, resulting in higher return and conversion rates.

Sometimes the direction is immediately obvious — a V-8 moment where the founder can’t believe they didn’t see it sooner. Other times it’s more layered. Either way, it takes an outsider to find THE TELL — and from there, to start working on a real solution.
